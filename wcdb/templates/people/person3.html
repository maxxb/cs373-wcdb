Mikhail Gorbachev


Mikhail Gorbachev, former President of the Soviet Union, wrote about the catastrophic accident in 1986 at Ukraine’s Chernobyl nuclear power station in an issue of the Bulletin of the Atomic Scientists. In his essay, titled “Chernobyl 25 Years Later: Many Lessons,” Gorbachev reflects on the Chernobyl disaster and writes how, two and a half decades later, the nuclear accident offers many lessons for preventing, managing and recovering from such a horrible event — he also writes about key issues that must be addressed for the further development of nuclear power.
